Cognitive Telemetry

Real-time alignment measurement

Definition

A measurement framework for assessing human–AI alignment in real time, modeled after performance diagnostics in technical systems.

What it looks like

Alignment visualized like telemetry charts — showing grip, drift, friction, feedback, and trend lines over time.

Overlaps

Anchors diagnostics within HARIL; complements Drift Layer and Signal Trace.

Importance

Shifts evaluation from ‘vibe’ to measurable signals, making alignment visible and actionable.
