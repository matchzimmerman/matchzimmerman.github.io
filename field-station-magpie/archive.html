FIELD STATION: MAGPIE is a living collection of recovered physical artifacts, interface systems, digital documents, and incomplete records. As Recovery Agents document new material, the archive—and the story emerging through it—continues to expand.

The collection is organized by material type. Recovered interfaces are presented first as systems, then as the retained iterative outputs produced by those systems.
