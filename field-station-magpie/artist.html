The project is built across media, then re-entered as evidence from within its own fictional archive.

The builder of MAGPIE is also one of its investigators.

Match Zimmerman works across physical artifacts, installation, sound, moving image, procedural code, narrative, and human–AI systems to construct—and then interpret—the archive.

01 / Role

Speculative Archivist

Zimmerman authors the world, builds its systems, fabricates its objects, and organizes its records.

02 / Practice

Across Media

Objects, paintings, tarps, books, sound, animation, code, and exhibitions function as connected archive fragments.

03 / Method

Human–AI Collaboration

Artificial agents operate inside a directed production and research framework shaped by human judgment and revision.

04 / Position

Inside and Outside

The artist is both MAGPIE’s real-world creator and an in-world researcher confronting evidence he does not fully control.
