Grip Index

Strength of intent/output alignment

Definition

A metric indicating the strength of alignment between user intent and AI output.

What it looks like

Outputs feel on track — the system holds direction tightly without slipping tone or focus.

Overlaps

Counterbalances Friction Index; relates to Cognitive Drift.

Importance

Provides a concrete measure of stability — whether the system grips the road of intent or starts to skid.
