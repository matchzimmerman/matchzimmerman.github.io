Cognitive Process Modeling

Training systems on how you think

Definition

The practice of training systems to reflect human reasoning methods — how we frame questions, iterate ideas, and refine outputs — rather than copying surface expression.

What it looks like

Solving new problems using your inquiry loops instead of completing sentences in your voice.

Overlaps

Directly tied to Recursive Interaction and Iterative Convergence; operationalizes SCIAs.

Importance

Provides the technical and conceptual backbone for building agents that extend cognition.
