Friction Index

Tension between prompt and response

Definition

A measure of the resistance or mismatch between intended prompt and model response.

What it looks like

Fluent text that ‘pushes back’ or veers, creating drag between intent and output.

Overlaps

Inverse relationship with Grip Index; interacts with Cognitive Drift.

Importance

Diagnoses whether misalignment stems from unclear input or systemic slippage.
